# Teto-Egen Content Calendar & Engagement Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive content calendar and engagement strategy for Teto-Egen Personality Test Web App, designed to drive user acquisition, engagement, and retention through strategic content marketing, social media engagement, and community building. The strategy aligns with business objectives and supports all user personas across multiple channels.

## Content Strategy Overview

### Strategic Objectives
1. **Awareness**: Build brand recognition and drive traffic (40% of content)
2. **Engagement**: Foster community and encourage platform usage (35% of content)
3. **Conversion**: Drive assessment completions and premium upgrades (15% of content)
4. **Retention**: Maintain long-term user engagement (10% of content)

### Target Audiences
1. **Career Seekers**: Professional development, workplace insights
2. **Self-Improvement Enthusiasts**: Personal growth, relationship dynamics
3. **HR Professionals**: Team building, hiring insights
4. **Students/Educators**: Learning styles, career planning

### Content Pillars
1. **Educational**: Scientific personality psychology, research insights
2. **Inspirational**: Success stories, transformation journeys
3. **Practical**: Actionable tips, how-to guides, tools
4. **Community**: User-generated content, discussions, events
